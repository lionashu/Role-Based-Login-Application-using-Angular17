How does Role Based Access System Works?

Angular's 17 Authguard work by restrictily access to certain routes or components based on the roles assigned to authentication users.

  • if User's role is Admin, they will be able to view all Users.
  • if User's role is Normal, they will be denied access.
  • if User's is not logged in, they will not be genrated access either.
